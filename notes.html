More than 25 years in the business

BWSC is more than just a windsurf school, it's an outdoor sports centre. Ranked among the world's top 5 and Europe's top 3 centers of its kind, BWSC was established in 1999 in th best location on Alacati's now-famous windsurf bay. BWSC is the area's first and now the biggest activity centre of its kind.

The BWSC windsurf school is a certified member of VDWS and the TYF. Over the last 25 seasons thousands of foreign and Turkish guests have successfully trained with our experienced and friendly VDWS certified instructors in English, German, French, Italian, Spanish and Turkish.
