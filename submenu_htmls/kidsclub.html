BWSC Kids Club

Kids Club Lessons

Introduce your kids to the joy of surfing with our fun and safe Kids Club lessons! Our experienced instructors are passionate about teaching kids the basics of surfing, from paddling and popping up to catching their first waves. We use specialized equipment and techniques to ensure a positive and rewarding experience for every child.

Lesson Options

Level Descriptions Duration Price
Beginner (Ages 6-8) Learn the fundamentals of surfing, including water safety, paddling, popping up, and balancing on the board. 1.5 hours $100
Intermediate (Ages 9-12) Build upon basic skills, learn to catch waves independently, and improve surfing techniques. 1.5 hours $120
Advanced (Ages 13-16) Focus on wave selection, turning techniques, and more advanced maneuvers. 2 hours $150

Pricing

All prices include equipment rental (softboard, leash, etc.) and instruction from certified instructors.

Location

Our Kids Club lessons take place at the safe and shallow waters of BWSC Alacati.
