BWSC Kitesurf Center

Kitesurf Lessons

Experience the thrill of kitesurfing with our expert instructors! We offer lessons for all levels, from beginner to advanced, tailored to your individual needs. Our proven teaching methods focus on safety and quick progression, so you can confidently enjoy the freedom of riding the waves in no time.

Lesson Options

Level Descriptions Duration Price
Beginner Learn the basics of kitesurfing, including safety, equipment setup, launching and landing the kite, and basic water starts. 2 hours $150
Intermediate Improve your kite control, learn how to ride upwind, and practice more advanced techniques like transitions and jumps. 2 hours $180
Advanced Master advanced kitesurfing maneuvers such as riding waves, performing tricks, and handling strong winds. 2 hours $220
Private Get personalized instruction tailored to your individual needs and goals, whether you're a beginner or an experienced kiter. 1 hour $100

Pricing

All prices include equipment rental (kite, board, harness, etc.) and instruction from certified instructors. Private lessons can be booked for any level.

Location

Our lessons take place at BWSC Alacati, a fantastic spot for kitesurfing.
