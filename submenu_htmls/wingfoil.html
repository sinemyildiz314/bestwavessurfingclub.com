BWSC Wing Foil Center

Wing Foil Lessons

Experience the freedom of flying above the water with our wing foiling lessons! Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your skills, our experienced instructors will guide you through the exciting world of wing foiling.

Lesson Options

Level Descriptions Duration Price
Beginner Learn the fundamentals of wing foiling, including equipment setup, basic wing control, water starts, and getting up on the foil. 2 hours $170
Intermediate Improve your wing handling skills, learn how to ride upwind effectively, and practice maneuvers like tacks and jibes. 2 hours $200
Advanced Master advanced wing foiling techniques such as riding waves, performing jumps and freestyle tricks, and handling challenging conditions. 2 hours $240
Private Receive personalized instruction tailored to your specific goals and skill level, with one-on-one guidance from our expert instructors. 1 hour $120

Pricing

All prices include equipment rental (wing, board, foil, etc.) and instruction from certified instructors. Private lessons can be booked for any level.

Location

Our lessons take place at BWSC Alacati , a fantastic spot for wing foiling with its consistent winds and flat water.
